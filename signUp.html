Is there a fee to open and maintain an account with Lightspeed?

There is no fee to open an account with Lightspeed. Maintenance fees include the following:

  • Lightspeed Trader software fee is your total monthly commission charges subtracted from $130. For example, if you incur $100 in commissions for a given month, your total software fee for that month will be $30 ($130 - $100 commissions = $30).
  • Market data is $13 for stocks and an additional $10 for options. Pricing for additional data can be found here.
  • If your account balance is below $15,000 and you do not incur at least $25 in monthly commissions then you will be charged a $25 monthly minimum inactivity fee.
  • All maintenance fees will be charged during the first business week of the month and will be based on commissions generated the previous month.

How do I found via ACH (electronic funding)?

ACH transfers initiated through your bank will be rejected. Lightspeed requires ACH relationships as well as ACH transfers to be initiated through the customer portal. If you do not know your login information, please contact a representative for assistance.

  • After logging in, go to “My Account” and click on the “Fund Transfers” link. ACH links and transactions initiated through other financial institutions will not be accepted
  • ACH transfers initiated through your bank will be rejected.
  • Once the ACH link is verified, ACH transfers take only 2 business days to be completed.
  • Lightspeed does not charge for ACH transfers.
  • ACH deposits are limited to $30,000 per 60 calendar days.
  • ACH links are limited to 1 per client.
